三种心情，三种打开方式 Three moods, three ways to play

想看江湖热闹，就进武侠酒馆；想被轻轻陪着，就进入心动陪伴；想立刻进入战斗，就启动星际突击队。 Enter Wulin Tavern when you want a lively wuxia crowd, choose Soulmate when you want something gentle beside you, or launch Star Fighter when you want action right away.

一个有江湖烟火气 One Feels Like Jianghu

武侠酒馆像一场随时开席的江湖盲盒，每次进门都可能遇到新的拼桌、新的八卦和新的神回复。 Wulin Tavern feels like a living jianghu lucky draw, where each visit can bring new tablemates, rumors, and sharp replies.

一个偏温柔 One Is Gentle

心动陪伴更像一段安静的日常，把聊天、表情和时间感做成轻轻的陪同。 Soulmate plays like a quiet everyday ritual, turning chat, expressions, and time into a soft sense of company.

一个偏热血 One Is Intense

星际突击队更强调手感和节奏，越往后越紧张，越想再来一局。 Star Fighter is about feel and rhythm: the longer you last, the tighter it gets, and the stronger the urge to try again.

AI Native 游戏制作流程 AI Native Game Creation Flow

从一句创意开始，把世界观、角色、玩法、美术、代码和上线验证串成连续循环。AI 不只是辅助写代码，而是参与灵感扩展、原型打磨、内容生成和体验迭代。 Start from a single idea, then connect worldbuilding, characters, mechanics, art, code, and launch checks into one creative loop. AI is not just a coding helper here; it shapes ideation, prototyping, content, and iteration.

适合快速把“想玩什么”变成“可以打开体验什么”，也适合持续扩展角色、剧情和玩法。 Built for turning “what would be fun?” into something playable, while leaving room to grow characters, stories, and mechanics over time.
